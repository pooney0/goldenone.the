The Golden One

This clothing brand was created to be simple, comfortable and wore by any one. We also created it to back to the community. We want the look of art to be ordinary but yet have its own character. By being ordinary, it gives the look or feel that leaves no one standing out. That each and everyone that wears an article of the brand brings everyone closer. Unlike the top brands like, Gucci, Louis Vuitton, Prada, where they have driven it to be about status or aimed at the richer side of of the consumers. We want to break that. We want our brand to be able to be bought by anyone and anywhere. By giving back to the community, The Golden One Co. , would pledge to give back a percantage of the profits. By helping the veterans who need their own help, helping the homeless get back on their feet and whatever else seems to fit best for the community.
